Kurta

In modern usage, a short kurta for women is referred to as the kurti. However, traditionally, the kurti refers to waistcoats,[48] jackets and blouses[49] which sit above the waist without side slits.

Bed sheets

A bed sheet is a rectangular piece of cloth used either singly or in a pair as bedding, being placed immediately below or above bed occupants. A top sheet, in the many countries where they are used, is a flat sheet.
